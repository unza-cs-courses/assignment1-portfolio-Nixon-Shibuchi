About Me

Hello! I'm Nixon Shibuchi, a passionate Computer Science student at the University of Zambia. I'm fascinated by software technologies and how they can be used to solve real-world problems. My journey into web development began with a curiosity about how websites work, which quickly evolved into a deep passion for creating responsive, accessible, and user-friendly applications.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python, and Java to build robust software and systems. I believe in writing clean, semantic code that not only functions well but is also maintainable and scalable. Beyond coding, I enjoy solving complex problems, collaborating with others, and continuously learning new technologies. As I progress through my Computer Science degree, I'm building a strong foundation in algorithms, data structures, and software engineering principles. My goal is to contribute to meaningful projects that make a positive impact while growing as a developer and team member.
